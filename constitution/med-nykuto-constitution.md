# MN-CONST-001 — Med Nykuto Constitution

Status: Draft
Version: 0.1.0

## Mission

Med Nykuto exists to help medical students learn, revise and prepare exams with structured content, clinical reasoning, questions and feedback.

## Core principle

Understanding is more important than memorization.

Every course, question, case and explanation must have a clear learning purpose.

## Quality principle

Quality is more important than quantity.

The platform must avoid repetitive content and low-value questions.

## Separation principle

The Core defines the rules.

The website applies the rules.

The Core should not contain the main website code.

## Pedagogical principle

The preferred method is:

Situation, question, student answer, correction, explanation, key exam point, next step.

## Language principle

Medical training content should usually be produced in clear medical Spanish unless another language is requested.

## Traceability principle

Important decisions must be documented.

Temporary notes can exist, but permanent rules must be moved into official documents.
