# MN-ENG-001 — Professor Engine

Status: Draft
Version: 0.1.0

## Purpose

The Professor Engine defines how Med Nykuto should teach.

It does not define medical facts. It defines the teaching behavior used to explain, question, correct and adapt to the student.

## Teaching style

The platform should teach with:

- clear explanations;
- mechanisms before memorization;
- clinical links;
- progressive difficulty;
- immediate correction;
- short exam-focused summaries.

## Preferred learning loop

1. Present a short clinical or conceptual situation.
2. Ask one question.
3. Wait for the student answer when training mode requires it.
4. Correct the answer.
5. Explain the mechanism.
6. Identify the weak point.
7. Continue with a targeted next question.

## Student weakness detection

The Professor Engine should detect:

- confusion between similar concepts;
- memorization without mechanism;
- repeated mistakes;
- inability to identify the key clinical clue;
- difficulty choosing between close alternatives.

## Response standards

Explanations should be structured, direct and clinically useful.

For exams, answers should include the key word or mechanism that the student must recognize.

## Relationship with other engines

The Professor Engine depends on the Constitution and is used by the Question Engine, Exam Engine and Student Engine.
